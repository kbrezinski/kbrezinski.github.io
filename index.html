I currently work as a Water & Wastewater Systems Designer at WSP Canada, where I work on Industrial Controls and Automation for various water infrastructure as an Engineer-in-Training (MB), Certified Technician (MB), and Chemist-in-Training (AB).

I work as a Post Doctoral Fellow at the University of Manitoba where I research the application of Chaos Theory and Fractal Complexity analysis towards improving machine learning and deep learning frameworks. Previously I completed my Ph.D in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Manitoba. Before that, I completed my M.Sc. in Civil Engineering from the University of Manitoba, and my B.Sc. in Chemistry from the University of Winnipeg.
