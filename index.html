I currently work as a Water & Wastewater Treatment Designer at WSP Canada, where I work on industrial controls automation for various water infrastructure as an engineer-in-training (EIT), certified technician (C.Tech.), and chemist-in-training (CIT).

Previously I completed my Ph.D in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Manitoba where my research interests focused on incorporating complexity into Deep Learning Architectures, Malware Classification and Combinatorial Optimization. Before that, I completed my M.Sc. in Civil Engineering from the University of Manitoba, and my B.Sc. in Chemistry from the University of Winnipeg.
